Chocolate-Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 Cups Plain Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 Cup Unsalted Butter, softened
  • 1/2 Cup White Granulated Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Light Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Cup Chocolate Chips

Method

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180 degrees.
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder and salt together.
  3. Mix the butter and sugar together until smooth and creamy in a seperate bowl.
  4. Slowly mix in the milk and vanilla extract to the butter.
  5. Add the flour to the wet mixture.
  6. Add the chocolate chips.
  7. Chill for 30 minutes
  8. Put a small scoop on a baking tray and bake for 10-12 minutes.
