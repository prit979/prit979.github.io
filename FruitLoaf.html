Fruit Loaf

Ingredients

  • 250g Self-Raising Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 110g Margarine
  • 110g Soft Brown Sugar
  • 2 tbsp Cornflour
  • 2 tbsp Yoghurt
  • 6 tbsp Milk
  • 225 mixed fruit

Method

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 150 degrees.
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder and the sugar together.
  3. Rub in the butter.
  4. Mix the cornflour, yoghurt and milk together. Mix into the flour
  5. Mix in the mixed fruit.
  6. Bake for 1 hour.
