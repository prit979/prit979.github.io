Palak Paneer

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Pack Spinach
  • Ginger
  • Chillies to Taste
  • Jeera
  • Cinnamon
  • 4 Cloves
  • 2 pods Cardamon
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • Kasuri Methi
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 1 Block Paneer
  • 1/4 tsp Garam Masala
  • 2 tbsp Double Cream
  • 1 tsp Butter
  • 3/4 tsp Salt

Method

  1. Boil the spinach till soft.
  2. Let the spinach cool down, then blend with the ginger and chillies till smooth.
  3. Melt the butter, then fry Jerra, Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamon, Bay Leaf.
  4. Add the tomatoes and saute till soft.
  5. Add the palak paste, salt and some water.
  6. Adjust the consistency, then add the paneer.
  7. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Add the garam masala and the kasuri methi.
  9. Add the cream, then ready to serve.
